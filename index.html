About EGAM Ministry

EGAM Ministry Vocational Training Centre is a faith-based Christian organization dedicated to empowering young people spiritually, academically, vocationally, and economically.


Through practical vocational entrepreneurship training, mentorship, and church-based leadership development, EGAM Ministry equips youths with skills necessary for self-reliance, innovation, and godly excellence.


The ministry also serves as a center for worship, community outreach, youth empowerment, business mentorship, and leadership transformation.

Vocational & Entrepreneurship Training

Computer Training

Practical ICT training including web design, graphics, programming, and digital literacy.

Entrepreneurship

Business development, startup mentorship, financial empowerment, and leadership skills.

Vocational Skills

Hands-on training in vocational crafts for sustainable youth empowerment.

Christian Leadership

Developing godly leaders through biblical mentorship, discipleship, and ministry development.
